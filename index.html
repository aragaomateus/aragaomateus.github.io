About Me

Computer And Data Science Student

As a Senior at NYU majoring in Computer and Data Science, my recent internship at Moises.ai as a Product Data Analyst has inspired me to concentrate on Data Visualization within my studies. This new focus enables me to translate complex analyses into actionable business insights. During my internship, I honed essential skills like self-motivation and accountability, particularly in a remote work environment.

Currently, I serve as an Intro to Python Programming tutor at NYU, a role that continuously challenges and refines my communication skills. I find great satisfaction in learning how to explain intricate concepts to beginner students, as these skills are highly transferable and invaluable in data analysis roles. I am also excited to join Amazon as a Data Engineer intern this coming summer.

Originally from Brazil, I have called NYC home for the past four years and have grown to love the city immensely. My five-year plan is to establish myself as a top-tier Data Scientist, renowned for delivering actionable insights to clients and stakeholders.
