About Me

I am currently a Senior studying Computer and Data Science at NYU. After interning at Moises.ai as a Product Data Analyst last summer, I have started to take a new direction within my data science studies, where I start to focus on Data Visualization and on how I can translate my analysis into actionable business insights. In this position, I also learned how to be e and how to be a self-starter and how to hold myself accountable working in a remote setting.

I am working as an Intro to Python Programming tutor at NYU. Being a tutor means being in a position that constantly puts one's communication skills to the test. I am in a continuous learning process of how to explain complex topics to beginner students. That is my favorite part of the job, because of how transferable and important this skill it is for jobs related to data analysis. In addition to that, I will be joining HashiCorp as Data Analyst intern during the summer.

Born and raised in Brazil, I have lived in NYC for four years, and this city has become one of my favorite places. My five-year plan is to become a top-tier Data Scientist that excels on delivering actionable insights to clients/stakeholders.
